## Overview  

**OmniFocus for Mac** provides a complete environment for planning, organizing, and managing your tasks and goals. Designed specifically for macOS users, it blends a clean, intuitive interface with powerful GTD (Getting Things Done) features — helping you move from chaos to clarity.  

With **OmniFocus 3 and 4**, you can capture every task instantly, group them into projects, and use smart tags to filter what truly matters. The app offers a unified workspace where personal and professional to-dos can live side by side, synced across all your Apple devices.  

The latest version, **OmniFocus 4 for macOS**, includes a modernized design, new widgets, enhanced automation, and tighter integration with Apple’s ecosystem — including Shortcuts, Focus Mode, and Siri commands. Whether you’re managing a team project or your daily schedule, OmniFocus ensures nothing slips through the cracks.  

It’s ideal for freelancers, entrepreneurs, and anyone seeking structure without friction. OmniFocus delivers the precision of professional project software wrapped in the elegance of a native Mac app.
